Our Story

A Café Born from
Rwandan Coffee

Akabaraza — meaning "the welcome" in Kinyarwanda — was born from a simple belief: Rwanda grows some of the world's finest coffee, and every cup should reflect that. From the terraced hills of the Western Province to your cup, we trace every bean's journey.

Our menu blends classic café favourites with Rwandan culinary traditions, from honey-glazed mandazi to avocado toast with isombe pesto, alongside our signature cold brew lineup.
