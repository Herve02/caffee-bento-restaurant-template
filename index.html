Our Story

A café born from
love of mornings

Akabaraza — meaning "welcome" in Kinyarwanda — opened in 2020 with one simple idea: a place where Kigali's people could start their day with intention. With a cup of single-origin Rwandan arabica and a plate of something made with care.

Every recipe on our menu has roots in Rwandan home cooking, reimagined for a modern café setting. We source our coffee from farms in the Eastern Province and our produce from weekly market runs across Kigali.
